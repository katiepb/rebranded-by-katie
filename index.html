About Me

Hi, I'm Katie 🤍

Senior at Rollins College · Collegiate Lacrosse Player · Disney Lover

I'm a senior at Rollins College, a collegiate lacrosse player, and someone who is always keeping up with the latest marketing and social media trends.

Originally from Annapolis, Maryland, I'm spending my summer in Winter Park, Florida, working as a Marketing & Brand Strategy Intern for Glass Slipper Concierge, where I work directly alongside Vice President Jennifer Kozlow on content creation, personal branding, and social media strategy.

Over the years, I've gained experience in hospitality, travel, marketing, and social media through internships with brands like Hilton and Marriott. Through each experience, I've realized what I love most: helping people tell their story and create a brand that feels authentic, polished, and uniquely theirs.

That's exactly why I started Rebranded by Katie — to help businesses create an online presence that matches the expertise, passion, and hard work behind their brand. Because first impressions matter, and your online presence should be just as impressive as the business you've built.

Mission

Many travel advisors are exceptional at planning vacations but struggle to create a social media presence that reflects the quality of their services. Rebranded by Katie helps advisors develop a cohesive, professional, and visually appealing personal brand that showcases their expertise, builds credibility, and elevates their online presence.
